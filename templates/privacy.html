Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Spendly is built around a simple principle: your expense data is yours. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have. We aim to be clear and concise, and to collect only what we need to provide the service.

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect the information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and a hashed password. As you use Spendly, we store the expense entries you record, including amount, category, date, and any description you choose to add.

We also collect a small amount of technical data automatically, such as your IP address, browser type, and timestamps of access. This information is used for security, debugging, and maintaining the reliability of the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect exclusively to operate Spendly. Specifically, we use it to authenticate you, store and display your expense records, generate summaries and category breakdowns you request, and keep the service secure and functional.

We do not use your expense data for advertising. We do not build advertising profiles. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in secure databases with access limited to authorised personnel. Passwords are hashed using modern algorithms — we never store them in plain text. Network traffic between your browser and Spendly is encrypted in transit using HTTPS.

While no system is perfectly secure, we take reasonable measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, alteration, or destruction. You can delete your account and all associated expense data at any time from your account settings; deletion is permanent and removes the records from our active systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly relies on a small number of trusted third party providers to operate — for example, a hosting service for our servers and an email provider for account-related messages. These providers receive only the information necessary to perform their function and are bound by confidentiality and data protection obligations.

We do not embed third party advertising or analytics trackers in Spendly. We do not allow third parties to set tracking cookies through the service.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You can access, correct, or delete your expense records at any time through the application. You may also export your data in a portable format. If you wish to close your account, you can do so from your account settings; we will permanently delete your personal data within a reasonable period after the request.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, or wish to exercise any of the rights described above, please reach out through the support channels listed in your account settings. We will respond to your request as quickly as we can.
